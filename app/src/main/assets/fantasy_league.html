Welcome

Welcome to Khelkund! An app that gives you your uninterrupted dose of Fantasy Cricket! Register yourself and get cracking with your very own Fantasy Cricket team, where strategy is the essence of the game.

Here are few rules, information and tips to help you get started with the game.

Getting Started

You can participate in the Khelkund Fantasy League by creating your own team. You will be given a budget of $100 million. Using this budget, you can pick your squad of 11 players from all the real life players you know and love, and show off your managerial skills. Once you create your team, while you concentrate on what’s happening on the pitch, we do the hard work of computing statistics and points on how each player in your team performs as per the scoring rules.

You can start with by clicking the Fantasy League text on the Home screen of the app.

Creating Your Team

Start by entering a team name (please note here that you can’t edit your team’s name once it has been created). You will also see a list of emblems on this page, you can select any one of these as your team's emblem.

Once you have entered the name and finalized on the emblem, you are ready to pick your playing XI.

Selecting Squad

You are provided with a budget of $100 million to buy players and add them to your squad. To add a player, you click on any empty grey tile. This will open a player pool page where you will see the actual players. Each player has a price associated with it, clicking on that player would add him to your squad and your balance would be reduced by that amount.

  • You keep on adding players until you have a complete playing XI. You can select one of the following formations for your team,
  • 6 Batsmen, 0 AllRounder, 1 WicketKeeper and 4 Bowlers.
  • 5 Batsmen, 1 AllRounders, 1 WicketKeeper and 4 Bowlers.
  • 5 Batsmen, 2 All Rounder, 1 Wicket Keeper and 3 Bowlers.
  • 4 Batsmen, 2 All Rounder, 1 Wicket Keeper and 4 Bowlers.
  • 4 Batsmen, 3 All Rounder, 1 Wicket Keeper and 3 Bowlers.

You can change the formation of your team at any time. To change formation you have to click change the change formation option on top of the screen when you are editing your team.

Choosing a Captain

You must choose one player among your squad as your marquee player or your captain. This player would give double the points to your team as he would normally score. You can make any player captain by clicking on its image once he has been added to your team. You will see an option to make that player as your captain once the details of that player open.

Transfers and Deadlines

The players in your team will score points for you in every match, so select your players wisely. You can make changes in your team by using your transfers.

Removing one player from your team and adding a new player to your team constitute as one transfer.

You will be given 80 transfers during the IPL league stage. An additional 10 transfers will be allotted once the knock out stages start. Note here that the league stage transfers won’t be carried over to the knock out stage.

You can make as many changes as you like before the first match of the IPL, without losing on any transfer.

Only the players who are in your team before a match starts will earn you points for that match.

You can change your captain as many times as you like without losing any transfers. Make sure you select a player as a captain who is actually going to play in that match so that you can score maximum points.

The deadline for saving your team for any match is the start time of that match. Any changes made once the match has started won’t be counted for that match and will only apply to the next match.
