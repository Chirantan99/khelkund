What is Pick’em 5?

Pick’em 5 is a new game introduced by Khelkund in this fantasy league series to help you win even more prizes. This game is played on a per match basis.

Getting Started

You can participate in a Pick’em 5 game for every T20 game in this series. In this version of game, you choose 5 players from the teams who are playing in a particular match.

You will choose 1 bowler, 1 batsman, 1 wicket keeper , 1 all rounder and 1 wild card player A wild card player can be of any type. For every player you choose, Khelkund will choose it's own opponent for your chosen player of the same type.

At the end of the match, each player will be awarded points, and if four of your players score more points than that of their assigned opponents, you will win.

What will I get if I win?

For every pick’em 5 match you win, an additional $10,000 will be credited to your balance in fantasy league. Using this balance, you can buy more expensive players and increase your chances of topping the global leraderboard, while also getting higher in your private leagues.

For every pick’em 5 win, you will also get 1 additional transfer added to your fantasy league team.

But remember, your balance can at most increase up to $1,01,00,000. And also, your transfers can at most increase by 20. So hurry up, pick your squad, and enjoy the game.
