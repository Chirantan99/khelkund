What is Private League?

Khelkund lets you play fantasy league among your friends. You can compete with your friends by either creating a new private league and inviting them or joining an existing private league.

Getting Started

Start by clicking on Private League on the Home Screen. You can create a private league by clicking the “+” button. Once you create a private league you can start sharing it among your friends to invite them to join.

Joining a Private League

To join a private league, enter the unique 5 digit code for that private league into the text box above. Once joined, you can click on any private league and view your position in that particular league.

Friend's Score and Rankings

You can see the rankings and score of your friends by clicking on any private league.
